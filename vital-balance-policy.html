Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16/01/2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Vital Balance App collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and health-related data when you use our application and services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and secure manner.

2. Data We Collect

When you authorize our application to connect with Garmin Connect, we may collect the following data only with your explicit consent:

2.1 Garmin Data

Depending on permissions granted, this may include:

  • Activity data (e.g. steps, distance, activities)
  • Heart rate data
  • Sleep summaries
  • Stress or wellness summaries
  • Timestamps and device metadata

We do not access raw sensor data unless explicitly provided by Garmin APIs.

2.2 Account & Technical Data

  • User identifier provided by Garmin (no Garmin password access)
  • Application logs (timestamps, error logs)
  • Device and platform information (OS, app version)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to:

  • Provide core application functionality
  • Display analytics, summaries, or visualizations to the user
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Troubleshoot issues and maintain system security

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls.
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized systems only.
  • We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • When necessary to provide core application services (e.g. secure cloud hosting)

All third-party services, if used, comply with applicable data protection regulations.

6. User Control & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Garmin authorization at any time via Garmin Connect
  • Request access to or deletion of your data
  • Stop using the application without penalty

Once access is revoked, we will no longer collect new data from Garmin.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
